ABOUT US



Our Journey

In 1972 entrepreneur Menna Hesham first met baker Omar Shedid in his small bakery shop in Alexandria, after she was astonished by the quality of his baked goods

and immediately she suggested the idea of starting a bakery and coffee shop with Omar in the streets of Cairo.

Within few months, it became a spot for famous actors and singers where they had breakfast and coffee on a usual basis.



Our Secret

With our premium Arabica coffee beans coming all thw way from Ethiopia, we grind them with our own special grinders to guarantee our dear customers the ultimate coffee experience.


The quality we maintain on our coffee and bakery is not only limited on providing the best taste but also achieving the highest industry health and safety standards help us achieve this quality by assuring hygiene 24/7 across all our branches.
